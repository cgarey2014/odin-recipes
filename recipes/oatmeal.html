Overnight Oats

Description

In this overnight oats recipe, almond milk and oats make a great no-cook oatmeal. Change it up with different almond milk flavors and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups Almond Breeze Vanilla almond milk
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 3 tbps coarsely ground chopped almonds

Steps

  1. Stir together almond milk and oats in a medium bowl
  2. Cover and refridgerate overnight or up to 3 days
  3. Before serving, top with granola and almonds, if desired
