Stove Top Chicken

Description

Steak might get most of the sizzle when it comes to the subject of meats, but the world's poultry fans love this simple Stove Top chicken recipe. Like its beefy cousin, chicken is extra magical if you follow a few simple rules.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cooking Oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat stovetop to medium high
  2. Add oil to pan
  3. Add onion to heated oil and sautee. Continue for 4 minutes
  4. Remove onion and set aside.
  5. Add chicken to pan and heat for 6 minutes, turning halfway through.
  6. Remove chicken and combine onion and chicken in bowl and serve.
