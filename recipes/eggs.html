Scrambled Eggs

Description

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 eggs
  • 4 to 6 tbps of milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley(optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs on flat surface and into a bowl
  2. Add milk to bowl, to thin the mix if desired
  3. Use fork to whisk mixture to perfectly fine
  4. Heat butter in non stick skillet over medium high heat
  5. When butter starts to bubble, add egg mixture and immediately reduce heat to medium low
  6. When eggs begin to set, gently stir with rubber spatula.
  7. Remove from pan and serve
